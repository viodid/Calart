German Cedano, Calat33 began his hobby about the drawing since childhood thanks to his mother was textile drawn. Then in adolescence he studied drawing and sculpture at the Lima Art Museum for later work in the cultural journal Urbania, making up the realization of the comic named Nick. Subsequently he traveled to Europe and exercised the work of street artist venturing the MIMO and the human statue up to that in 2016 driven by The economic need went down to the sand of the shell beach to experiment with drawings in large dimensions.

From that moment he is dedicated to the sand art professionally, perfecting his drawing technique in the sand with garden rakes, and eventually becoming its main source of Inspiration and expression.

He uses the words to express the emotions of him and leave a nice memory of the visitors of the bay.
